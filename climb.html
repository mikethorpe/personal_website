Climbing...

My love of climbing began in 2010 when I started visiting indoor walls. I improved quickly progressing to climbing outdoors, an altogether more serious activity. I was taken by the physicality of moving over rock, the sheer calm, meditative state it induces in me and how it can teach one to deal with danger.

For three years of my climbing career I worked as a professional climbing coach, instructing beginners and experienced climbers alike.
