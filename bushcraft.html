Bushcraft adventures...

"Going to the woods and playing with knives and fire" was how I recently described bushcraft. Whilst that's not far from the truth there's more of an art to it than that. It's the skill of using natural resources to live outdoors, something that requires you to engage with and become more aware of our natural world.

Inspired my Ray Mears, colleagues and friends I love to spend time improving my firemaking, carving and survival skills...
